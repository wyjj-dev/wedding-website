FAQs
Where is the wedding?
Our special occasion will be held at Ember beach club, which is part of One&Only Desaru resort.
What is the itinerary?
Kindly arrive before 4pm to get settled down. The ceremony will start at 4.30pm, followed by photo taking around 5pm. Canapés and drinks will be provided during such time. Dinner will start at 7.30pm and ends till late.
Is transport provided?
Unfortunately, transport is not provided. We are able to arrange some kind of transport for you but payment will be on your own. There are ferry services between tenah merah ferry terminal to desaru ferry terminal. We will be able to pick you up from the different hotels in desaru to our wedding if you do not have any means of transport.
Where am I staying and do I need to pay?
There are various hotels available in Desaru, Hardrock Hotel, Westin, Anatara and Airbnb etc. If you do like to stay in One and Only Desaru, do let us know and we will try to get the best rates for you. Sad to say, it will be on your own cost.
What are the transport available in Desaru if I don't have any car?
There is a lack of taxi and grab services in Desaru. However, we are able to arrange some pick up services for you to bring you to our wedding in One&Only Desaru.
Dress code?
Do wear semi formal, short sleeve shirt and your gorgeous dress in this few colour snow blue, lavender, light rose, baby pink. The ceremony will be by the beach and dinner is in an open setting, do avoid thick clothes!
