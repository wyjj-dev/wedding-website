Where is the wedding?

What is the itinerary?
行程是什么？
Kindly arrive before 4pm to get settled down. The ceremony will start at 4.30pm, followed by photo taking around 5pm. Canapés and drinks will be provided during such time. Dinner will start at 7.30pm and ends till late.
请您在下午4点前到达，以便安顿下来。仪式将在下午4点30分开始，拍照将在5点左右进行。期间将提供小吃和饮料。晚餐将在晚上7点30分开始，一直持续到很晚。
Is transport provided?
是否提供交通服务？
Unfortunately, transport is not provided. We are able to arrange some kind of transport for you but payment will be on your own. There are ferry services between tenah merah ferry terminal to desaru ferry terminal. We will be able to pick you up from the different hotels in desaru to our wedding if you do not have any means of transport.
